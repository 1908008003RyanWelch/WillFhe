# WillFHE

**WillFHE** is a privacy-preserving, blockchain-based platform for creating, managing, and executing encrypted digital wills and testaments.  
By leveraging **Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE)**, it enables conditional execution of assets based on encrypted criteria, maintaining privacy while ensuring immutability and trustless operation.

---

## Project Overview

Estate planning and digital wills are critical tools for ensuring assets are distributed according to a userâ€™s wishes.  
However, conventional approaches face significant challenges:

- **Privacy risks:** Digital or on-chain wills often expose sensitive personal and financial data  
- **Trust issues:** Executors or intermediaries may manipulate or delay distribution  
- **Complex conditions:** Execution depends on verification of external events, such as death or legal approval  

WillFHE solves these problems by providing a **secure, encrypted environment** for conditional execution using FHE, ensuring assets are allocated only when specific conditions are met without revealing the underlying details.

---

## Motivation

Traditional will execution systems can lead to:

- Unauthorized access to personal or financial information  
- Vulnerability to disputes over inheritance details  
- Complexity in verifying conditions like death, age, or other legal milestones  

WillFHE enables:

- Automated, condition-based execution  
- Fully encrypted asset allocation logic  
- Protection of sensitive estate data from third parties
